Hi Everyone,

My name is Rugile Januskeviciute and I am the author of this website.

I grew up in a country that not everybody knows about- Lithuania! I was blessed to be growing up in such a nature-filled country and I finished high school in the capital- Vilnius, which I am from originally.


After finishing high school I decided that Vilnius is not enough for me and I want to do something more crazy and exciting. So I decided to completely change my life. After studying really hard for the high school exams, one morning of August 2018 I received a letter congratulating me on getting into the University of Manchester to study BCs Biomedical Sciences. I was beyond happy!

So, I packed my two big suitcases and there I was, alone, but very excited for the new adventure in Manchester.

Right now I am a second-year student, about to go into my industrial placement to work in the research laboratory. This time at the university has allowed me grow, become more independent and find friends that made me even happier than I was.


With this website I want to share my journey with you, and hopefully you decide to come along!


Let's collaborate!
